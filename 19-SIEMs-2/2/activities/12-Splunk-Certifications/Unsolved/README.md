## Activity File: Splunk Certification

- For the remainder of the class, you will register for and begin the Splunk Fundamentals Certification.

- You must registering for a Splunk account and complete the certification registration.

- Once the registration is completed, you will complete the first three modules of the Splunk Certification.


### Setup and Registration

1.  Navigate to the [Splunk registration page]( https://www.splunk.com/page/sign_up?redirecturl=https://www.splunk.com/).

    - Fill in the registration information located on the right side of the page, titled **Create Your Splunk Account**. 

2. Navigate to the [Splunk Fundamentals 1 page](https://www.splunk.com/en_us/training/courses/splunk-fundamentals-1.html). 
   
    

   - Select **Register** under the eLearning Section.
   - Agree to the terms and conditions and select **Register**.
   - Select **Checkout** at the bottom of the next page.
   - Login with your account if prompted.
   - On the confirmation page, agree to the terms and conditions and select **Confirm Order**. 
   - Select **Take me to my Learning Dashboard**. 

### Starting the Certification Modules

1. Select **Start Course** under **Splunk 7.x Fundamentals Part 1 (eLearning)**.

  - **Note:** It may take 5 - 10 minutes to start the course after registering.

2. Complete Modules 1 - 3 of the Splunk Fundamentals Certification:
   
    1. What is Machine Data?
    2. What is Splunk?
    3. Installing Splunk
